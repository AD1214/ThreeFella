Langzhong Ancient City Langzhong Ancient City (Lang zhong City), referred to as Langzhong, also known as Langyuan, Ba Zi Guo, is located in the northeastern part of the Sichuan Basin, as of December 2014, covers an area of 1,878 square kilometers, is a national 5A-level scenic tourist area, and with Lijiang in Yunnan Province, Pingyao in Shanxi Province, and Shexian in Anhui Province, and is known as China's best surviving "four great ancient cities ". -Consisting of Wide Alley, Narrow Alley and Well Alley, it is an ancient street of the Qing Dynasty that still exists in Chengdu in a more complete scale. -Broad Alley has the largest and most complete collection of old buildings in the whole neighborhood, where you can enjoy a bowl of tea, eat authentic Sichuan cuisine and experience the customs of old Chengdu. -Narrow Alley has both late Qing Dynasty buildings and early Western-style foreign buildings, which is a leisure and life taste area mainly focusing on Western-style food culture, art and leisure. -Jing Alley is a typical epitome of folklore Chengdu, in addition to the fascinating brick culture wall, it also gathers Chengdu's special snacks, folk stuffs and so on. -The Giant Panda Breeding and Research Institution, which completely simulates the wild living environment of giant pandas, is built with lakes, streams, bamboo forests, lawns, etc., and houses more than 100 giant pandas in total. -There is a giant panda museum near the main gate, and you can send postcards with panda postmarks at the Panda Country Post Office and watch documentaries about giant pandas in the movie theater. In addition to pandas, you can also see small animals such as black-necked cranes, peacocks and herons. The Sun Maternity Ward and the Moon Maternity Ward concentrate the most adorable newborn panda tuanzi, a few months old, are very cute and highly recommended! -Dufu's former residence in Chengdu, where he lived for nearly four years and composed hundreds of poems, is regarded as a "holy place" in the history of Chinese literature. -The present Dufu Cao Tang was restored many times during the Song, Yuan, Ming and Qing dynasties, and the pavilions and platforms in the garden are lined with ancient trees. -The main attractions include the light house, the Hall of Poetry and History, the Shrine of the Ministry of Public Works, etc. The "Du Poetry Calligraphy and Woodcarving Gallery" displays more than a hundred pieces of Du Poetry Calligraphy and Woodcarving works. -Dufu's thatched cottage, which was restored with the characteristics of western Sichuan houses, has become the landmark of Dufu's Cao Tang.
