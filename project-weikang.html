BEI JING XI AN CHENG DU Beijing is the capital of China. There are many ancient Chinese buildings here, the most special one is the Forbidden City. When you come to China, you must go to the Forbidden City to experience the ingenuity and wisdom of the ancient Chinese. Chengdu, China is a very wonderful city. The world-famous spicy hotpot is in Chengdu. Chengdu people are very fond of spicy food. If you like spicy food, you may want to go to Chengdu to try the spicy hot pot! XI AN Xi'an is the capital of Shaanxi, and there are many world cultural heritages here. The tomb of China's first emperor is located in Xi'an. If you are very interested in Chinese history, you may wish to travel to Xi'an. In addition, there are many tourist attractions in Xi'an. When you come to Xi'an, you may also want to go to the famous temple called Famen Temple, where cultural relics have been unearthed for a lifetime. That is the Buddha's finger relic. The Buddha's finger relic is a sacred object of Buddhism. If you are lucky when you come to Famen Temple, you may be able to see the Buddha's finger relic.
